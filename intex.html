The Challenge
\n

Gulf businesses receive hundreds of leads daily across multiple channels (web forms, WhatsApp, email). Manual triage and follow-up consume 40+ hours per week, with 30% of leads going cold before first contact.

\n

Trigger

A new lead message arrives — web form, WhatsApp, or email.

Process
\n

The system scores, routes, and works the lead without manual triage. AI evaluates lead quality, detects competitor mentions, logs to CRM, attempts WhatsApp contact, and falls back to email. Human takeover flags high-value opportunities.
